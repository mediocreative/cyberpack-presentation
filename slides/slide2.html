STEAM Subject Overview

A Comprehensive STEAM Education Journey (Ages 10-18)

British System: Years 6-13 American System: Grades 5-12

Our STEAM Subject curriculum provides a structured, year-by-year progression, guiding students from foundational concepts to advanced applications.

Spanning Years 6-13 / Grades 5-12 (Ages 10-18), the program develops a comprehensive set of skills across key technology domains.

Each stage builds upon the last, culminating in capstone projects and real-world problem-solving.
