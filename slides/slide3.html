CYBERPACK Kit Usage in Clubs

STEAM Clubs utilize the CYBERPACK Advanced Kit(s), providing all necessary components for the 32 core projects across 4 difficulty levels. The club environment emphasizes in-depth study, customization, integration of multiple projects, and development of unique solutions beyond standard instructions.
